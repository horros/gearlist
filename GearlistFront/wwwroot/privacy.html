Privacy policy

TL;DR: We at Gearlist 💖 privacy. We will never sell your information to any third party. We will never collect any data about you beyond the bare minimum to be able to technically operate the service.
Email us and we'll show you exactly what data we have stored on you.

What data do we collect?

When logging in using a local account, Gearlist stores a technical id (subject) and email address. Your email address is used when you log in.
IF you log in using a federated identity provider (that means an external ID, so eg. Google, Facebook etc) your name will be automatically received and stored in Azure AD B2C. Work is in progress to remove that.

How do we collect your data?

You directly provide the data we collect. We collect data when you:

  • Sign up or sign in to the service, either using local accounts or external identity providers
  • Save data into the system

How do we use your data?

Your data is only used for logging you in and linking data you actively save into the system to your account.

You will receive an email if you opt-in to receiving notifications when people find your stolen gear (when that feature becomes available).

How and where do we store your data?

Gearlist does not maintain any infrastructure itself. Your user account related data is stored in Microsoft's Azure AD B2C. The data you save is stored in an Azure Cosmos DB database. The API uses Azure Functions. Images you upload are stored in an Azure Blob storage. All of the Microsoft-operated services are located within the West Europe region, except for Cosmos DB whose read/write region is Switzerland North. Switzerland has received an "adequacy decision" from the European Commission which means Switzerland's privacy laws are compatible with GDPR.

All communication from your browser onwards and between all services is encrypted using TLS encryption.

Marketing

We will never sell your email address or any of your data to anyone. We will never send you any marketing related email. We will never contact you unless with opted-in features (see "How do we use your data") or as required by law in case of a data breach.

Data protection rights

You are always entitled to the following:

  • The right to access: You have the right to request copies of your data. At a future date there will be a button in the user inteface for this.
  • The right to rectification: You have the right to have incorrect data rectified.
  • The right to be forgotten: You have the right to request that all your data is deleted. At a future date there will be a button in the user interface for this.

If you make a request we have one month to respond to you. We will of course do our best to respond as soon as possible.

If you wish to exercise any of these rights, please write to us on admin@gearlist.cloud.

Cookies

We use cookies for keeping you signed in. You may disable cookies in the browser, but the site may not work correctly.

For more information about cookies, see allaboutcookies.org.
